Data model foundation for storage observability and quotas: QuotaBytes field on Share model, incremental atomic usage counters in all metadata store implementations, and BlockStore.Stats() returning real physical size.

Purpose: All downstream plans depend on having (a) a QuotaBytes field to store per-share limits, (b) accurate O(1) logical usage counters in the metadata layer, and (c) real physical size from the block store. This plan creates the data layer that quota enforcement, protocol reporting, and CLI/API observe.

Output: QuotaBytes field on Share model (GORM auto-migrated), usedBytes atomic.Int64 counters in memory/badger/postgres metadata stores with delta tracking on all size-changing operations, BlockStore.Stats().UsedSize wired to LocalDiskUsed, and unit tests for counter accuracy and stats correctness.
